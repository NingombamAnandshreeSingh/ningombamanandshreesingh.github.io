About me

A Brief Introduction

I, Ningombam Anandshree Singh, completed my Ph.D. from the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur), under the supervision of Prof. Sudip Misra in Smart Wireless Applications and Networking (SWAN) Lab. During my Ph.D., I received the prestigeous Dept. of Science and Technology (DST) -– INSPIRE Research Fellowship. Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor II at the School of Computing, DIT University. Prior to that, I completed my M.Tech degree and B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Manipur, India. I am the first position holder in our M.Tech. batch (2014-2016, NIT Manipur).


Area of Interests

My research interests include Internet of Things (IoT), Camera-Networks, Cloud Computing, Edge Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Camera-Network-as-a-Service.
